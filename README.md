# Netflix-DataAnalysis
Netflix data analysis using Python, Pandas, and Matplotlib 
# 🎬 Netflix Data Analysis Project

## 📌 Overview

This project performs data analysis on the Netflix dataset to uncover patterns, trends, and insights related to content distribution, genres, ratings, and growth over time.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Analyze Netflix content (Movies vs TV Shows)
* Identify trends over time
* Understand country-wise distribution
* Explore genre popularity
* Derive meaningful business insights

---

## 📁 Dataset

* Source: Netflix Movies & TV Shows Dataset
* Rows: 8800+
* Columns: 13

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning

* Handled missing values (director, cast, country, rating)
* Converted `date_added` to datetime format
* Extracted numerical values from `duration`
* Removed duplicates

---

## 📊 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

* Univariate analysis (type, rating, country)
* Bivariate analysis (type vs duration, year vs type)
* Trend analysis over years
* Genre distribution analysis

---

## 📈 Visualizations

* Bar charts (Movies vs TV Shows, Ratings, Countries)
* Line plot (Content growth over years)
* Histogram (Movie duration distribution)

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Netflix has more Movies than TV Shows
* Content increased significantly after 2015
* United States dominates content production
* Drama and Comedy are the most popular genres
* Most content targets adult audiences (TV-MA, TV-14)
* Movie duration mostly ranges between 90–120 minutes

---

## 📌 Conclusion

The analysis shows that Netflix focuses heavily on movies and has rapidly expanded its content library in recent years. The platform emphasizes popular genres and targets a mature audience, with strong content contributions from the United States.
